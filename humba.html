Humba Bisaya

Ingredient Quantity
*4 servings
Pork belly 2 pounds *1-1/2 inch cubes
Vinegar 1/4 cup
Soy sauce 1/2 cup
Pineapple juice 1 cup
Oil 1 tbsp.
Small onion 1 *thinly sliced
Garlic 6 cloves *minced
Peppercorn 1 tsp.
Bay leaves 2
Can of tausi 1 can *drained & rinsed
Pineapple chunks 1 cup
Dried banana blossoms 1 oz.
Brown sugar 1 tbsp.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix pork, vinegar, soy sauce, and pineapple juice. Marinate in the refrigerator for 30 minutes. Drain the pork, keeping the marinade.
  2. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Cook onions and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the pork and cook, stirring occasionally, until lightly browned.
  4. Pour in the reserved marinade and let it boil for 2–3 minutes without stirring.
  5. Stir in peppercorns, bay leaf, tausi, pineapple chunks, and banana blossoms.
  6. Lower the heat, cover, and cook until the pork is tender.
  7. Add brown sugar and stir until dissolved. Season with salt and continue cooking until the sauce thickens and starts to render fat.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
