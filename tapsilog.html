Tapsilog

Ingredient Quantity
*3 servings
Beef sirloin *thinly sliced 1 lb.
Eggs 3 pcs.
Cooking oil 3 tbsp.
Knorr liquid seasoning 6 tbsp.
Garlic 6 cloves *crushed
Pineapple juice 3/4 cups
Brown sugar 2 tbsp.
Ground white pepper 1/4 tsp.
Leftover rice 5 cups
Salt 1 tsp.
Garlic 5 cloves *crushed

Instructions

  1. Place the thinly sliced beef sirloin in a large bowl. Combine it with the marinade ingredients: Knorr Liquid Seasoning, crushed garlic, pineapple juice, brown sugar, and ground white pepper.
  2. Mix well, cover, and refrigerate overnight to marinate.
  3. Heat 3 tablespoons of cooking oil in a pan. Add the crushed garlic and sauté until it turns light brown.
  4. Add the leftover rice and stir-fry for about 3 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and continue to stir-fry for another 3–5 minutes. Set aside.
  6. Heat a pan and pour the marinated beef, including the marinade, into it. Add ¾ cup of water and bring to a boil.
  7. Cover and cook until the liquid reduces to half.
  8. Add 3 tablespoons of cooking oil and continue cooking until the liquid evaporates. Fry the beef in the remaining oil until medium brown. Set aside.
  9. Fry sunny-side eggs using cooking oil.
  10. On a large plate, arrange the beef tapa, garlic fried rice, and fried egg.
  11. Enjoy!
