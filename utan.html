Utan Bisaya

Ingredient Quantity
*4 servings
Eggplant 1 pc. *sliced
Okra 10 pcs.
Ginger 2 thumbs *sliced
Tomato 1 pc. *wedged
Green onion 5 stalks *sliced
Spinach/Alugbati 1 1/2 cups
Squash 1 1/2 cups *cubed
Malunggay leaves 1 cup
Long green pepper 2 pcs.
Fish fillet 2 slices
String beans 1 to 2 cups *2 inches
Patola 1 cups *sliced
Water 2 cups

Instructions

  1. Heat water in a pot until it comes to a boil.
  2. Add the ginger and squash to the boiling water. Cover and let it cook for 8 minutes.
  3. Stir in the string beans, scallions, okra, and tomato. Let the liquid return to a boil, then add the long green pepper and eggplant.
  4. Add the fish to the pot. Cover and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in the patola and malunggay leaves. Cover and let cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Season with salt and add spinach just before finishing.
  6. Transfer to a serving bowl and serve warm. Enjoy and share!
